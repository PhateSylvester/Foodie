GUIDES

Here's a simple recipe to make a delicious burger:



Ingredients:

1. Ground beef (or preferred protein)
2. Salt and pepper
3. Burger buns
4. Lettuce
5. Tomatoes
6. Cheese (optional)
7. Onions (raw or caramelized)
8. Pickles
9. Mayonnaise or ketchup
10. Mustard (optional)
11. Bacon (optional)

toppings you might consider:

1. Avocado
2. Grilled mushrooms
3. Grilled bell peppers
4. Jalapeños or hot sauce
5. Caramelized onions

PREPARATION

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Patties:

- Season the ground beef with salt and pepper.
- Form into patties (about ¾ inch thick).


2. Cook the Patties:

- Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
- Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired doneness.


3. Assemble the Burger:

- Toast the burger buns.
- Add lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and any other toppings.
- Spread mayonnaise or ketchup on the bun.
- Place the cooked patty on the bun.


4. Serve:

- Serve immediately and enjoy!


Tips:



- Use fresh ingredients for the best flavor.
- Don't overwork the meat when forming patties.
- Let the patties rest for a minute before assembling the burger.
