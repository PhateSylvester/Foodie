Ingredients used in preparing jollof-rice

#ingredients:

  • 2 cups long grain parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, blended
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • Chicken stock
  • Salt, Seasoning cube, Pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, green beans)-(optional)

How to prepare a delicious jollof-rice

#Preparation:

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and set aside to drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the blended tomatoes and pepper, garlic, and ginger. Cook for about 10 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  4. In the tomato paste, add thyme, curry powder, and white pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add your chicken stock, salt, and bay leaf. Bring to a boil.
  6. Add the drained rice and mixed vegetables (if using). Stir well.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot with foil then the lid (to seal in steam).
  8. Cook for about 30 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
  9. Fluff with a fork and serve hot with fried plantains or protein of choice.
