Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a thin, long, solid, cylindrical pasta that was
created by the Italians which is popular in different places around the world.

Recipe for spaghetti

  1. Spaghetti noodles
  2. Vegetable oil (Especially virgin olive oil)
  3. Garlic
  4. Onions
  5. Tomato sauce
  6. Salt and black pepper
  7. Seasoning powder
  8. Curry leaves
  9. Rosemary
  10. Grounded beef or any type of meat
  11. Thyme

How to cook spaghetti

  • Boil the water: Fill your pot with water and add salt and oil
  • Add the spaghetti: Add the spaghetti and stir to avoid sticking to each other
  • Cook until soft: Check the spaghetti and taste to see if it is soft - it should be tender but slightly firm
  • Strain: Pour the spaghetti into a colander and sieve it
  • Make the sauce: Use the vegetable oil to fry the tomato paste, then after pour water and add the ingredients
  • Mix them all: Mix the spaghetti and your sauce and stir over low heat until the flavor mixes

    • Best way to serve spaghetti

  • Fresh & Fragrant:- Use fresh basil leaves and a dash of lemon juice to give it a fragrant finish
  • Rich & Savoury:- Use chesse(probably parmesan or pecorino romano) to give it a salty and sour aroma
